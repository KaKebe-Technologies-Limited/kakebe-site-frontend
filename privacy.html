Privacy Policy for Oroma TV

Effective Date: 16th May 2025

At Oroma TV, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is protected. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you access or use the Oroma TV application (the “App”), which streams shows, events, conferences, news, and current affairs across Northern Uganda.

By downloading or using the Oroma TV App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

a. Personal Information

We do not require registration to view most of our content. However, if you choose to create an account or interact with certain features (e.g., personalized settings, saving favorites, providing feedback), we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Location information (if permission is granted)

Users can exercise the following rights regarding their personal information:

  • Request that their data we collect be deleted.
  • Delete their account, after which no record is kept with us.
  • User data is not shared outside the company.

b. Device and Log Information

When you access our App, we may collect technical data including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Browser type (if accessed via smart TV browsers)
  • Timestamps and duration of use
  • Pages and features accessed

c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and performance tracking. You can disable cookies through your device settings, although this may affect functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To deliver and optimize content streaming services
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve performance
  • To develop new features and enhance user experience
  • To monitor and prevent unauthorized use or abuse of the App
  • To communicate updates, promotions, or relevant content (if subscribed)

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • With third-party service providers strictly for hosting, analytics, or technical support under confidentiality agreements
  • If required by law, subpoena, or legal process
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of Oroma TV, its users, or the public
  • During business transitions such as a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, with notice provided

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Anonymous usage data may be retained for analytical and research purposes.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit
  • Firewall and server protection
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Despite our efforts, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security. Users are advised to use strong passwords and keep their devices secure.

6. User Control and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing (e.g., marketing communications)

7. Children’s Privacy

The Oroma TV App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is unintentionally collected, it will be promptly deleted upon discovery.

8. Third-Party Content and Links

Our App may contain links to or embed content from external websites or services. These are not controlled by Oroma TV, and their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of third-party platforms.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the App or on our official website. The updated policy will always reflect the latest effective date.
